List of Olympiads

SISO

The Springfield International Science Olympiad (SISO), a test of competence and knowledge in the subject of Science, is held based on syllabus prescribed by CBSE/ ICSE and the State Boards.

SIMO

The Springfield International Mathematics Olympiad (SIMO), a test of competence and knowledge in the subject of Mathematics, is held based on syllabus prescribed by CBSE/ ICSE and the State Boards.

SIEO

The Springfield International English Olympiad (SIEO), a test of competence and knowledge in the subject of English, is held based on syllabus prescribed by CBSE/ICSE and the State Boards.

SICO

The Springfield International Cyber Olympiad (SICO), a test of competence and knowledge in the Cyber Domain, is held based on syllabus prescribed by CBSE/ICSE and the State Boards.

Grads Olympiad

The Grads Olympiad is one of its kind Olympiad, primarily focusing on MBA aspirants. This is an online multiple-choice question based Olympiad.

Finance Olympiad

The Finance Olympiad is a specially curated Olympiad, made with a purpose to inculcate the habit and increase the knowledge of personal finances in a fun way.

FAQ's

What is the syllabus for the Olympiads?
For students of class 3 to 12, the syllabus of the Olympiads is the same as followed by the CBSE, ICSE and various state boards.\r\nFor Grads and Finance Olympiad, the syllabus is specific to the Exam you choose to register for. You can visit the respective olympiad pages to know more about the syllabus.
Do I get a certificate of participation?
Yes, Certificates are provided to all the participant along with various cash prizes, medals, merit certificates to the top rankers!
What are the benefits of taking the Olympiads?
  • Increase your knowledge
  • Strengthen your Resume
  • Stay updated on the current trends
  • Preparing for your placements
  • Preparing for any other type of competitive exams
  • Enhance your knowledge and skills
  • Also, Participants receive state and national level ranking along with Prizes that include cash awards, medals, gifts, certificates
What are the resources/books I can use to prepare?
For students of class 3 to 12, no special preparation is required. All olympiads are designed in such a way that anyone can comfortably participate just by studying their school syllabus. For the finance olympiad, you'll need to be updated with personal finance trends.\r\nFor Grads Olympiad too, no special preparation is required. It is based on reasoning, aptitude and grammar.
What is the mode of conduction of the exam?
All Exams are conducted completely in online mode
